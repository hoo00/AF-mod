GTF ENFORCER

Type:  Standard Police Fighter
Maneuverability:  High
Hull Armor:  Heavy
Manufacturer:  Vega Corporation
Length:  17 meters
Gun Mounts:  4 Mounts (2,2)
Missile Banks:  2 Banks
Description:  Rapid Response and Tactics


The GTF Enforcer is the standard issue of the GTVA Police. It can rival almost any military fighter in most aspects including agility and fire power plus it is heavily armored using the latest technology. Most of the law enforcers who are ex-military pilots compared this ship to the Perseus interceptor.
