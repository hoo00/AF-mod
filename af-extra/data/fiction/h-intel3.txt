UPC SCORPION

Type:  Heavy Assault
Maneuverability:  Average
Hull Armor:  Heavy
Manufacturer:  Unknown Deneb Shipyards
Length:  17 meters
Gun Mounts:  8 Mounts (4,4)
Missile Banks:  2 Banks
Description:  UPC Heavy Fighter


The pride ship of the United Pirate Coalition which is intended to mock the GTVA military. The Scorpion-class fighter is agile and fearsome with firepower boasting to rival the Erinyes class. Many GTVA police have lost their lives engaging these crafts and it is indeed a nightmare to peace and order.
