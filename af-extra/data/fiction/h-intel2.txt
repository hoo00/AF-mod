UPC RAIDER

Type:  Recon
Maneuverability:  Good
Hull Armor:  Light
Manufacturer:  Unknown Deneb Shipyards
Length:  20 meters
Gun Mounts:  4 Mounts (2,2)
Missile Banks:  1 Banks
Description:  UPC Recon Fighter


The UPC Raider is a fast and agile fighter with similar handling and capabilities of the GTF Loki fighters except that it is very hard to target due to its body configuration and hull color. Ever since the United Pirate Coalition captured the Deneb system and its facilities, the pirates have the capabilities of manufacturing their own arsenal.
